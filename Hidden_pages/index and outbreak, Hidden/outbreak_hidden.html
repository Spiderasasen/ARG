First calls with the CSIS and CIA tech liaisons. Interesting folks. The CSIS team seemed... overly specific about certain backend access permissions. Didn't quite understand why they needed control over how certain file types are handled, but hey, they're the clients, right? Just making notes.


May 15th, 2024

Things are getting weird. Director Daniel Rogers from CSIS initiated some big alterations to their section of the site today. We’ve tried to replicate it, checked the audio files, checked the network traffic – nothing obvious. Alex is usually rock-solid, but he's pretty shaken up about it. "It's tied to the click," he insists. We need to figure out what that sound is and where it's originating.
